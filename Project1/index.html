Real-Time Monitoring and Alerts

Develop a system that uses sensors, drones, or satellite imagery to continuously monitor crops for signs of disease. Provide real-time alerts to farmers when potential issues are detected. This can help in early intervention and reduce crop losses.

Disease Diagnosis and Identification

Offer a diagnostic service where samples (e.g., leaves, soil) are analyzed using advanced techniques such as DNA sequencing, machine learning algorithms, or lab tests to accurately identify the specific disease affecting the crops. Provide detailed reports and recommendations for treatment.
